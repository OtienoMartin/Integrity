V-Int Stands for Vehicle Integrity.
We develop this app to boost passager safety each time they use a public a service vehicle.
This app is aimed at minimizing road accidents as passengers are able to tell which vehicles are road worthy in terms of servicing, maintenace and insurace validity.
Status of the vehicle including maintenace record is given in the app. There is also a provision for passengers to report Vehicles that are non-compliant.
